About Me

I'm an art history graduate that's discovered the fascinating world of coding and web development. After the pandemic, I sought a career change with resilient sustainability with the freedom to work remotely. My love for travel with my fiance has recently unearthed a passion for photography. I often use my five pets (2 cats, 2 dogs, 1 hedghog) as subjects to experimenting with point of views.
